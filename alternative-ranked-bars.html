Alternative Design: Dual Ranked Bar Charts

Comparing OECD countries by life expectancy (left) and health spending (right). This alternative design focuses on ranking and relative positioning rather than correlation.

Design Rationale

This alternative explores ranking-based comparison. Each country appears on both charts, allowing viewers to see relative positions in both dimensions. Hover over a country to highlight its position on both rankings.

Strengths

  • Clear individual country rankings
  • Easy to identify top and bottom performers
  • Familiar bar chart format
  • Good for mobile/narrow screens

Limitations

  • Harder to see correlation between variables
  • Doesn't show quantitative relationship
  • Visual clutter with 39 countries
  • Limited analytical capabilities vs. scatterplot
